Features

  • notifications_activeNative Notifications
    Fully native notifications on macOS, on Windows and Debian.
  • queue_musicCustom Sounds
    Choose from the existing selection or import your own.
  • content_pasteUniversal Clipboard
    Copy & paste across devices (requires Pushbullet Pro).
  • mmsRich Notifications
    Push thumbnails and channel icons for apps like IFTTT.
  • phonelinkMirroring
    Mirror phones notifications (Android only).
  • photo_size_select_smallUnobtrusive
    Low resource footprint. Runs in the menubar or system tray.
  • snoozePower Snooze
    Pause notifications for one, four or eight hours.
  • call_mergeOpen Source
    MIT licensed. Developed and managed on GitHub.
